About Me

Respected and trusted U.S. Army Special Forces Green Beret and results-driven leader with an MBA (~2020) and MS in Biotechnology seeking to capitalize on an eclectic set of skills and expertise operating across complex and diverse multi-faceted cross-functional environments. Appointed to numerous leadership and management positions in fast-paced, high-stress and dynamic working environments leading dozens of personnel requiring both exigent and routine task management. Team focused people- first leader with a strong reputation for solving complex and wicked problem sets. Subject Matter Expert in short and long-term mission planning and analysis, with skilled project and program management, research and development, procurement, and multimillion-dollar budgeting experience. Knack for building rapport with an exceptional ability to communicate throughout all levels of a business to drive bottom-up input for accurate and timely decision-making. Prior to joining Snap Inc. Corey spent 14 years working in the Department of Defense and Intelligence Community. He began his career in the U.S. Army Special Forces as a Green Beret where he trained, advised, and led foreign partners. After leaving the military he held numerous roles within the Intelligence Community, which covered all aspects of, and responsibilities for, the planning and execution of intelligence, operations and security. His education includes an M.S. in Biotechnology from Johns Hopkins University, a B.A. in Intelligence Studies from American Military University, and is currently completing an MBA at Cornell University.
